I hold an MSc with Distinction from the University of Oxford in Social Science of the Internet, and a Bachelor's with Honors and Distinction from Stanford University in Symbolic Systems (Human-Centered AI).

I've worked as a business analyst at McKinsey & Company, focusing on (responsible) artificial intelligence, and in Product & Analytics at the Interaction Company of California. I have written for the San Francisco Giants as part of Vox Media, and hold a minor in Poetry.
